Welcome to Study Time Scheduler

This web application helps you manage your study schedule efficiently. Add your study subjects, set the time needed, and assign a priority. The system uses a priority queue to automatically schedule your most important tasks first, so you can focus on what matters most and avoid last-minute stress.

  • Add, update, delete, and view your study tasks
  • Automatic scheduling using a priority queue (min-heap)
  • Mark tasks as completed
  • Switch between dark and light mode for your comfort

Get started by adding your first task or viewing your schedule!
