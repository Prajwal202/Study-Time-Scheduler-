About Study Time Scheduler

The Study Time Scheduler is a web-based application designed to help students efficiently manage their study schedules. By leveraging a priority queue (min-heap), the app ensures that the most important tasks are always at the top of your schedule, helping you avoid last-minute stress and improve productivity.

  • Enter study subjects, time needed, and priority for each task
  • Automatic scheduling using a priority queue
  • Easy-to-use interface for adding, editing, deleting, and viewing tasks
  • Modern design with dark/light mode support
